About Me

I'm a dedicated student at the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy (IMSA), where I've immersed myself in advanced STEM studies while pursuing my passion for music and community service. As a 2025 FRC World Championship qualifier and state math competition champion, I combine academic excellence with practical engineering skills.

My journey spans from founding nonprofit organizations to conducting cutting-edge research, always with the goal of using STEM to make a positive impact on underserved communities. Through my work with GirlCon, PowerYou, and Harmonic Youth Ensemble, I've learned that the most meaningful innovations come from combining technical expertise with genuine care for others.

PowerYou Podcast

PowerYou: An Insight Into Success

Made by youth, for youth

As co-founder of PowerYou, I launched this youth-led podcast alongside my friends Jacob and Vivian to provide free academic resources and inspiration to pre-college students. Our mission is to share authentic experiences and practical advice from peers who understand the challenges of navigating academic and personal growth.

Through PowerYou, we bridge the gap between ambition and achievement by offering first-hand perspectives on diverse topics that matter to young people today.

What We Cover

🎓 Academic Paths & Pre-Law Career Exploration
🏃‍♂️ Youth Sports & Leadership (Track & Field, Hockey)
🔬 Science Fair Strategies & STEM Experiences
🎨 Music, Art & Creative Development
🌟 Personal Development & Success Insights
🤝 Community Service & Social Impact

Our Mission

We believe that the best insights come from peers who are actively navigating similar challenges. By sharing our diverse experiences in academics, sports, arts, and community service, we aim to inspire and inform the next generation of leaders.
